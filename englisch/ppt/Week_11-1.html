# History

In Chinese history, three dominant ideologies are the most representative: feudalism, the Three Principles of the People and socialism. The most prominent ideologies in the West can be listed as six: imperial thought in Ancient Greece and Rome (after the establishment of the city-state), theology, humanism, rationalism, capitalism, socialism. It was in the 19th and 20th centuries that thousands of âismsâ appeared in the West, mostly at the level of general spirit, culture and art, but rarely at the level of ideology, which was determined by their relationship with social structure and political system. The most representative ideologies in the world are capitalism and socialism. The two ideologies have consolidated and evolved in relation to the regime. The socialist ideology has become an important part of the socialist system and interpersonal relations. [3]

# Definition

Ideology is the spiritual form of a certain political community or social community. Ideology shows its significance and function in political community and social community, so many foreign studies on ideology often put it in the political structure and social structure, or in political science and sociology. A political party and regime, especially in modern times, is inconceivable without ideology. Its party constitution, political program and system not only define the nature of ideology, but also the product of ideology.

# Status

The status of ideology is determined by the superstructure of political community and social community. It is an important part of the superstructure to which it belongs. The status, function and power of the superstructure determine the status, function and power of ideology. The superstructure should not only reflect the economic basic system, but also be located in the decision-making, power, power protection, self-establishment and improvement of the upper echelon of the whole society. Therefore, the ideology it belongs to not only has the significance of directly or indirectly interacting with the economic basic system, but also has the significance of directly generating benefits for the consolidation, development and improvement of the superstructure.

# Constituent elements

The constituent elements of ideology mainly refer to the constituent elements of its spiritual form, which is the expansion of the spiritual attributes of ideology and the definition of its connotation.

# Superstructure

There are various ways for ideology to embody the relations of power and regime, including guiding, guiding, channeling, supervising, attacking, destroying and eliminating. For the spiritual exploration workers who support the superstructure of the spiritual circle and its regime and power relations, ideology mainly uses the way of guidance and guidance to ignite their creative enthusiasm, so as to serve the expansion and deepening of ideology. For the spirit workers who can unite and are not in the same line temporarily, the way of channelling is used; For the emergence of various ideas and works at any time, often in the way of supervision; For non-ideological and anti-ideological people and opinions, especially when they threaten or even destroy ideology, in many cases, they will strike, destroy or destroy their spiritual banner, maintain the superstructure to which they belong, and embody their will to power through other forces of the superstructure.

# Basic spirit

On the other hand, ideological connotation is the basic spirit within the scope of its spiritual form itself. This is a particular aspect which is connected with the political superstructure, but which is quite different. As the spiritual form of the superstructure, ideology has a very wide range of contents and numerous categories, stipulating that its spiritual essence should not only be correspondingly extensive, but also highly concentrated.

# Anti-ideology

The social level of anti-ideology has a specific meaning. It also has a distinct and determined ideology, but it is not orthodox, it is anti-orthodox. Orthodoxy refers to the dominant ideology in the social structure and is considered to be correct and authentic. Those who oppose such dominant ideology which is considered to be correct and authentic generally belong to the anti-ideology category.

# Strategic tasks

To build a socialist ideology with strong cohesion and guidance, we must also keep pace with The Times, keep to the current, and constantly improve the quality and level of our work. The object of ideological work is human beings, and it is to work in the ideological and spiritual fields of human beings. We must study the law of activities of human thoughts and spirits, study the relationship between changes in peopleâs ideas and social environment and educational guidance, find the pulse of social thoughts, and grasp the topics, contents and key points of publicity and education. We should better combine ideological work with the new changes in peopleâs spiritual life. [4]

# United States

From the end of the 20th century to the beginning of the 21st century, the ideological polarization of the contemporary United States appeared. The ideological polarization of the United States is increasing. The left-wing liberalism based on the Democratic Party, which emphasizes free trade, feminism, legalization of same-sex marriage, egalitarianism and globalization, is in sharp contrast to the right-wing conservatism based on the Republican Party. On the other hand, right-wing conservatism is polarized by its views on trade protection, white supremacy, anti-homosexuality, anti-immigration, and anti-globalization. The ideological polarization of the contemporary United States is distributed in various fields, including democratic socialism and conservatism in the economic field, party polarization, elite polarization and popular polarization in the political field. The key areas of polarization are still in the cultural aspect. Ideological polarization is very serious in various fields such as race, morality, religion, education and language. At the same time, this polarization also unfolds in the level of global communication, forming an all-round picture of ideological polarization. The ideological polarization of the contemporary United States has a very complex root, among which the main reason is the decline of the leading role of liberalism. [7]
